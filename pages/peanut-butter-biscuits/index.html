Park Imminent Recipes Peanut butter biscuits

Ingredients

  • 200 g sugar
  • 110 g butter
  • 250 g peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp vanilla essence
  • 190 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp soy milk

Method

Preheat oven to 190°C or 180°C fan forced.

Cream butter and sugar, then mix in peanut butter and vanilla essence.

Add remaining ingredients, mix and shape into small balls. Press with fork.

Bake for 15 minutes.
